Moe's Tavern

A food ordering application written in Ruby on Rails. Designed after The Simpsons "Moe's Tavern".

This was the first time working as a team. We had four on the team and had to come up with a plan together. I'm very proud of the design we came up with for this one as I did most of the front end planning and work.
